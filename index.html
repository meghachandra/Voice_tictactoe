Voice Tic Tac Toe

Instructions

To play Voice Tic Tac Toe, use the following commands:

  • Say "[position]" to play as Current Player:X (By default).
  • Say "[position]" to play as Current Player:O.

Available positions:

  • Top Left
  • Top Middle
  • Top Right
  • Middle Left
  • Center
  • Middle Right
  • Bottom Left
  • Bottom Middle
  • Bottom Right

Player X Wins: 0

Player O Wins: 0
